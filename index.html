Welcome to Our World

Imagi Nations is a bespoke educational storytelling service created for home education families and independent learners.

Our professional Storytellers create interactive role-playing adventures inspired by fantasy, mythology, history, mystery, science, and your child’s imagination.

Every session can be tailored to your child’s interests, educational goals, learning pace, and preferred subjects. Whether your focus is English practice, creative writing, history, science, mythology, confidence building, or nurturing a love of learning, the adventure adapts around your family.

Using collaborative storytelling and virtual tabletop technology, learners explore magical worlds, solve mysteries, overcome challenges, and grow in confidence within a safe and supportive environment.

Educational Benefits

Learning feels most powerful when children are actively participating in the story.

Vocabulary & Language

Children naturally encounter and practise new vocabulary through dialogue, puzzles, descriptions, role-play, and creative problem-solving.

Creative Thinking

Adventurers solve challenges, make meaningful choices, and explore imaginative solutions throughout each session.

Reading & Listening

Interactive storytelling encourages attentive listening, comprehension, narration, and confidence in communication.

Memory & Focus

Remembering characters, clues, locations, and story events strengthens short-term and long-term memory.

Social & Emotional Learning

Role-playing different perspectives helps children develop empathy, communication skills, and emotional understanding.

Confidence Building

Children are encouraged to share ideas, make choices, and express themselves creatively in a supportive space.

How the Adventure Begins

Every programme is custom-built around your child, your educational goals, and the themes that excite your family.

Family Consultation. We discuss your child’s interests, confidence level, educational focus, and preferred themes.
Educational Alignment. We weave in subjects such as English, history, mythology, science, creative writing, social development, or problem-solving.
Vocabulary Integration. Key words and learning themes become part of the story through dialogue, puzzles, magical artefacts, and interactive challenges.
Story Customisation. Adventures may include dragons, pirates, mysteries, historical quests, enchanted forests, fantasy kingdoms, or your child’s favourite ideas.
Adventure Delivery. The final session is delivered online through Foundry Virtual Tabletop with interactive maps, characters, and guided storytelling.
Continued Growth. Parent and learner feedback helps future sessions evolve alongside the child’s learning journey.
